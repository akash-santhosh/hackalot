I had a craze for electronics and communication devices since my father and mother were related to the field. My Mother was among the first people in Kerala to study programming languages,nut she never taught me anything about computer. But my Father spent most of the time mentoring me Electronic Circuits and Components,he is a Cable Operator and Local Internet Provider. I was transitioned from Electronics to Internet and IT Field since my Father Started providing various types of internet services to customers. When i started my High School,i met some good guys.
